Sev Bed Time Routine

Brush Teeth

Materials

Remove Partial Dentures

pull up on bottom denture clasp - (lower right)
pull down on top denture clasp - (upper left)

Rinse & Place in Container with Tablet of Cleaner

Cover & Soak Overnight

Brush Teeth

Rinse Toothbrush & Tub & Return to Drawer

Move to Bed

Attach Safety Belt = Sit-to_Stand Harness

Buckle Belt & Pull Tight

Re-Tighten Belt After Partial Raise

Stand Up From Chair & Move to Bed

Pull Down Pants & Sit On Bed

Unbuckle Safety Belt
Tell Him to "Let Go of Handlebars & Pickup Feet"

Move & Park Sit-To-Stand & Plug In to Charge Battery

Undress

Raise Bed
Remove Shoes, Pants & Socks > Place in Laundry Basket

Remove Sweater > Hang in Closet

Tell & Show Him Where His Pillow Is
Tell Him to Lay Back & Put His Head on the Pillow
Assist Him to Lay Back & Swing His Feet Up Onto Center of Bed

Get Products Ready On Table

Fill Bucket 2/3 Full With Warm-Hot Water

Peel Brief Open and Clean Front With Soap & Water
Dry & Apply AntiFungal

Clean BackSide

Have Him Grab Opposite Rail With Right Hand
Roll Him Over Onto His Side

Remove Brief & Clean Backside As Required
Use Wipes For Stool
Finish With Soap & Water

New Brief & Cup Cath Ready

Place Clean Brief Under Rear & Tuck Tab & Edge Under

Tuck CuP Cath Belt Under With Brief Tab

Apply Butt Paste On Butt
Use Wound Gel On Open Sores

Move Bucket & Bag With Soiled Brief to Kitchen Floor
Return Other Products To Shelf

Tell Him To "Let Go of Rail and Fall Back on His Back"

Tell/Assist Him "To Grap Near Rail Using Left Arm/Hand"
Roll Him Over Slightly to Pull Out the Brief Tab and the Belt that was Tucked Under

Tell Him to "Let Go of Rail and Return to Flat on Back"
Place Cup Cath Over Front Genitals & Connect Belt
Unwind Leg Straps
Tell Him to Lift His Knee & Thread Strap Under Leg and Button on Side of Bikini
Repeat with Other Leg & Strap

Lift Near Leg/Foot and Place Drain Tube/Hose Under Leg Near Knee Crease

Tell Him to "Spread Legs Apart" and Pull Brief Out & Fold Up and Over Cup

Stretch Brief Wings & Fasten Tabs

Place Pillow Under Calfs-Ankles - Feet Heels Exposed

Make Sure Cath Drain Hose/Tube is Straight to Bag

Hang Bag Under Bed & Place Tub Under Bag
Make Sure Bag Drain Valve Is Closed

Raise Foot of Bed & Head of Bed Slightly
Place Wedge Pillow Under Lower Back Butt (Near Side)

Cover Up & Adjust Pillow Under Head
Ask Him If He's Comfortable? & Warm Enough?
Tell Him Goodnight & Turn Off Lights

Close Pocket Door to Kitchen
Take Bucket to Laundry Tub to Rinse
Place Wash Rag & Towel In Wash Machine

Put Waste Bag with Soiled Brief & Wipes In Bag In Trash Can In Garage

Go Back & Wipe Bed Control, Sit-to-Stand, Table, & Touched Surfaces With Disenfecting Wipe
